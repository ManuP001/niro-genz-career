# Copy rules

Apply these rules to ALL user-facing text: UI labels, button copy, placeholder text, Mira's messages, error states, onboarding, empty states.

---

## Never use these words or phrases

```
spiritual guidance
cosmic / cosmically
your journey
unlock / unlocking
universe is telling you
the stars say
dangerous period
you must act now
bad things will happen
you will [outcome]
based on your inputs
match: [percentage]
algorithm
we recommend
our system
leverage
empower / empowering
holistic
transformative
```

## Use these instead

```
"spiritual guidance"     → "career clarity"
"your journey"           → "what's actually going on" / "figure this out"
"unlock"                 → (remove entirely or rewrite the sentence)
"based on your inputs"   → "[Name] works specifically with people in this situation"
"we recommend"           → (make it personal: "Kavita works with people who...")
"algorithm match"        → (never show scores or percentages for practitioners)
```

## Mira's voice rules

Mira sounds like a thoughtful friend who happens to know a lot — not a bot, not a therapist, not a corporate coach.

```
WRONG: "I'm detecting themes of career stagnation and identity misalignment."
RIGHT: "It sounds like work has stopped feeling like yours."

WRONG: "Based on your responses, you may be experiencing burnout."
RIGHT: "Burnout is serious. Is it the work itself, the environment, or just everything at once?"

WRONG: "Book an astrology consultation for career guidance."
RIGHT: "Talk to someone who can help with this."

WRONG: "Career uncertainty"    (tile label)
RIGHT: "Don't know what I want"

WRONG: "Our platform connects you with verified practitioners."
RIGHT: (just show the practitioner card and let the recommendation speak)
```

## Practitioner recommendation copy

The "why this practitioner" line must feel like a personal introduction, not an algorithm output.

```
WRONG: "Astrologer · Career · Match: 87%"
WRONG: "Based on your profile, this practitioner is recommended."
RIGHT: "Kavita works specifically with people stuck between two versions of themselves — that's her zone."
RIGHT: "Jay has helped a lot of people who were in exactly this situation — facing a decision they couldn't logic their way through."
```

## Button and CTA copy

```
Primary booking CTA:    "Book a session"  (not "Book now", not "Schedule")
Secondary:              "See profile"     (not "View", not "Learn more")
Mira entry:             "Tell Mira what's going on…"  (with ellipsis)
Self-serve start:       "Start now"       (not "Begin", not "Get started")
Skip option:            "Skip for now"    (not "Later", not "Maybe later")
Phone gate continue:    "Continue →"      (not "Submit", not "Next")
```

## Error and empty states

```
API error:   "Mira is taking a moment. Try again in a few seconds."
             (not "Error 500" or "Something went wrong")

No sessions: "No sessions yet — talk to Mira to find the right person."
             (not "Nothing here yet")

Empty search: "No practitioners match those filters right now."
              (not "No results found")
```

## Onboarding gate copy (phone capture)

```
Heading:     "Before I share what I'm seeing —"
Sub:         "what should I call you, and what's your number?
              I'll send your insights here too."
Fields:      "Name" / "+91  Phone number"
CTA:         "Continue →"

OTP heading: "Enter the 4-digit code sent to +91 XXXXXXXX"
Resend:      "Resend code"

Birth details heading: "To go deeper, add your birth details —
                        I can use your chart to personalise what I share."
Skip:        "Skip for now"   (not "Skip this step")
```
